P K Kunjalikutty

Current Cheif Minister of Kerala

Started his political journey through the Kerala Students Union (KSU). He later became active in Youth Congress politics and gradually built a strong base in North Paravur. He has been elected multiple times as MLA from the Paravur constituency.

Before becoming Chief Minister, V. D. Satheesan served as the Leader of the Opposition in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He became known for aggressive debates against the LDF government, especially on corruption allegations, governance failures, and administrative transparency.

V. D. Satheesan was born in Ernakulam district, Kerala. He studied law and has a background in student activism. He is widely recognized for his strong oratory skills and structured parliamentary debates, which significantly increased his visibility in Kerala politics.
